Privacy Policy

Runway respects your privacy, especially because financial data is inherently sensitive. We aim to handle product-related information with restraint, transparency, and clear boundaries.

Local-first No sale of personal data Plain-language disclosure

1. Information we collect

Runway is centered around financial information you choose to enter or import, such as transactions, categories, asset values, cash flow, and scenario inputs. This information is used to present your current financial state, calculate runway and trend indicators, and support on-device viewing, editing, and management.

2. How data is stored

In the current product design, Runway prioritizes local storage. In practice, that means core financial records are intended to remain on your device in support of tracking, analysis, and projections.

If future versions introduce cloud sync, account systems, or remote backup, we will update this policy before those capabilities are released and explain the change inside the product.

3. How we use the information

  • To display assets, cash flow, baseline spending, trends, and runway-related metrics
  • To support on-device record keeping, editing, organization, and review
  • To help with troubleshooting, support communication, and product improvement when necessary

4. Whether we sell your data

No. We do not sell your personal information or financial data, and we do not treat data sales as part of the product model.

5. Third-party services

If the current or future product uses crash diagnostics, performance analytics, authentication, payments, or other third-party services, we will limit that use to what is necessary and minimize the data shared where possible. If a third-party service processes your personal information, we will disclose that in this page or inside the product.

6. Your control over data

You can review, modify, or delete the information you enter on your device. If future versions introduce accounts or cloud features, we will provide corresponding guidance for access, correction, export, or deletion.

7. Children's privacy

Runway is not designed for children. If you believe a minor has provided personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy if product capabilities or data practices change. Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how product-related information is handled, please contact:
support@fuhailab.com
