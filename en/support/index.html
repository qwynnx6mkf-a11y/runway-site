Support

If you run into an issue while using Runway, want to share feedback, report a bug, or ask about a product detail, you can reach us by email. Support should feel as clear and calm as the product itself.

Public support email App Store ready Feedback welcome

Contact

Developer: Fuhai Lab
Support email: support@fuhailab.com

Helpful details to include

  • Your device model and OS version
  • The steps that led to the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings when relevant
  • If the issue involves ledger data, trends, or runway calculations, a small sample or description of the numbers involved

Scope

Troubleshooting

We can help identify UI issues, display anomalies, interaction problems, or gaps in product understanding.

Product feedback

We welcome suggestions about language, hierarchy, clarity, and the overall emotional quality of the experience.

Response expectations

We aim to respond within a reasonable time, though we do not promise immediate replies.
