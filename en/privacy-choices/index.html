Privacy Choices and Data Requests

If you want to ask about Runway's handling of personal data, or you need help with access, export, deletion, or related privacy requests, you can contact us through the details on this page.

Access and export questions Deletion guidance Public contact path

What you can request

  • Clarification on whether a category of data is stored locally on device or sent to a third-party service
  • Questions related to personal information, diagnostic data, or support records
  • Future access, export, or deletion requests if account-based or cloud features are introduced

Important note about the current version

The current design of Runway follows a local-first approach, with financial records primarily intended to remain on your device. If you are not using account, cloud sync, or remote backup features, many data actions may be more appropriate to complete directly on the device itself.

How to contact us

Email: support@fuhailab.com
To help us respond more efficiently, please include the device you are using, the OS version, the page or feature involved, and the action you would like to complete.
