Privacy Policy

Runway handles information tied to personal finance, which means privacy is not a minor setting at the edge of the experience. We aim to be explicit about what the product touches, how it is intended to behave, and where the limits of the current version still are.

Local-first direction No sale of personal data Plain disclosure of future changes

1. Information the product handles

Runway is designed around information you choose to enter or import, including transaction records, categories, asset values, cash flow inputs, baseline spending assumptions, and scenario data used for forecasting.

These records exist so the product can help you understand your present state, read direction over time, and estimate runway or other planning-oriented outputs.

2. Local-first storage intent

The current product direction is local-first. In practical terms, core financial records are intended to remain on your device to support tracking, analysis, and projections without assuming a remote service as the default source of truth.

If future versions introduce account systems, cloud sync, or remote backup, we will update this policy before those capabilities are made available and explain the change in clear language.

3. How information is used

  • To display assets, cash flow, baseline spending, trends, and runway-related metrics
  • To let you create, edit, organize, and review financial records on your device
  • To support troubleshooting, support communication, and product improvement when needed

4. Sale of personal data

We do not sell your personal information or your financial records. Data sale is not part of the intended product model for Runway.

5. Third-party services

If the current or future product uses third-party services for crash diagnostics, analytics, authentication, payments, or similar operational needs, we will aim to minimize what is shared and disclose the role of those services in the product or in this policy.

6. Your control over records

You can review, modify, or delete the records you enter on your device. If future versions add account-linked storage or cloud features, we will provide corresponding guidance for access, correction, export, or deletion.

7. Children’s privacy

Runway is not designed for children. If you believe a minor has provided personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us.

8. Changes to this policy

If the product’s capabilities or data practices change, we may update this policy. Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy or product-related data handling can be sent to support@fuhailab.com.
